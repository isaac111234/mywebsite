About Us

...Transforming Ideas.

The Power-365 Entrepreneurial Show was developed by a global team of decent, creative people and was founded in the entrepreneurial City of Lagos. Power-365 is a forum for African entrepreneurs seeking long-term success on both the local and international levels. We follow innovative models in our attempt to create a better society for everyone. We exist to help entrepreneurs surmount difficult business hurdles.

Our Aim

To be a veritable means of support for businesses lacking access to proper funding.

To reduce unemployment in different areas by funding startups and existing businesses.

Our Mission

To contribute our quota towards the push for economic stability through developing stable and self-sustaining structures that aid economic growth.

Our Vision

To ease the difficulty experienced by entrepreneurs in acquiring much-needed business funding.

Brief History

Nelson Sunday created the African tech-entrepreneurship program called Power-365 to help entrepreneurs realize the possibilities that come with their businesses and ideas, and to provide solutions to some of the most obvious problems facing humanity today.

We believe that only those who have once been at the forefront of similar challenges through their rich vein of experience would know how best to tackle such challenges and their possible consequences in ways that would be generally beneficial.

Our e-Team

Our e-Team is made up of highly skilled professionals with experience in different fields. They will educate, guide, advise, and also monitor the progress of the businesses of the beneficiaries.

Those who are selected will be enlightened on the proper usage and efficient management of funds provided to them by the company. Such businesses will be provided with structural designs to aid effective business management.

Selected entrepreneurs will also be assisted in the area of business registration with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and other business regulatory bodies in order to provide their businesses with a corporate identity. Above all, the e-TEAM ensures that all beneficiaries abide by the company's "ME2" rule.

A Description of Our Show

  • The Power-365 Entrepreneurial Show is an audio-visual program that provides seed funding for entrepreneurs.
  • Entrepreneurship has helped nations tackle economic problems. The success of startups and SMEs will go a long way towards achieving economic stability on a national scale.
  • This is why we have developed a program that is aimed at resolving the financial crisis faced by entrepreneurs today.
  • Starting a business can be very difficult, especially when you begin with a loan. Even loans can be quite difficult for new companies and entrepreneurs to access, and there is the ever-present pressure on you to succeed or else forfeit your valued collateral.

    • The object here is to provide you with non-refundable capital for your business or idea.
